# Abstract

The GOsasun project presents a comprehensive healthcare management platform designed to streamline interactions between patients, healthcare workers, and administrative staff. Developed using a dual-platform approach, the system integrates a PHP-based web application for patient accessibility and a C#-based desktop application for specialized clinical and administrative tasks. A significant feature of the desktop portal is its ability to interface with real tensiometers to capture accurate blood pressure and pulse data via XML, alongside the management of weight and height metrics. These integrated clinical records facilitate thorough patient follow-ups and allow for the attachment of relevant diagnostic documentation. The development methodology ensures data integrity through a relational SQL database utilizing advanced views, triggers, and indices. Overall, GOsasun demonstrates the potential of modern technologies to enhance healthcare efficiency and patient engagement by providing a unified, secure, and highly configurable environment for medical data management.
